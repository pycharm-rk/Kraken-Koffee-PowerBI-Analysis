This project features a Power BI dashboard created to analyze the Q1/Q2 sales performance of Kraken Koffee's new Florida flagship stores (Orlando, Tampa, Miami).

## üöÄ Key Achievements & Goals

* **Total Sales (Half-Year):** $6,98,812 from 1,49,116 transactions.
* **Average Order Size:** $4.69.
* **Full-Year Sales Forecast:** Projected to hit $14,09,207.

## üìä Dashboard Insights

The dashboard is built across three sections to answer key business questions:

### 1. Product Performance & Revenue Drivers
* **Top Category:** **Coffee** is the top revenue driver ($270K, 38.6% of sales).
* **Top Product by Revenue:** **Davy Jones' Sustainably Grown Organic Hot Chocolate Lg** is the strongest seller overall.

### 2. Temporal Analysis & Operations
* **Peak Time:** The busiest time for sales is around **10 AM**.
* **Day of Week:** **Monday** is the highest revenue day, and Saturday is the lowest.
* **Actionable Insight:** Management is re-evaluating opening/closing times due to slow sales before 7 AM and after 7 PM.

### 3. Sales Forecasting & Projections
* **Average Daily Sales:** Spiked from $2,852 (Jan-Mar) to $4,859 (Apr-June).
* **Stretch Goal:** The goal is $15,92,839, based on sustaining recent growth.
